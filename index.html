Common Questions

Shadow Slave FAQ

What is Shadow Slave about?

Shadow Slave is a dark fantasy web novel by Guiltythree. It follows Sunny, a young man who survives the First Nightmare and gains the Shadow Aspect. He must navigate a world of Nightmare Creatures, ancient ruins, and deadly politics while hiding his true power behind a demonic mask. With over 2,400 chapters, it's one of the most popular progression fantasy novels.

Who are the main characters?

The main characters include Sunny (The Dreamless — Shadow Aspect protagonist), Nephis (Changing Star — Undying Flame), Cassie (Blind Seer — prophetic visions), Effie (Hunt Aspect warrior), Kai (Nightingale archer), and Morgan (Valor). RIFT tracks all 300+ characters with full profiles.

What is the nightmare rank system?

The rank system goes: Dormant (Sleeper) → Awakened → Ascended (Master) → Transcendent (Saint) → Supreme (Sovereign). Each rank requires surviving increasingly dangerous Nightmare trials. Sunny currently holds the Saint rank. Explore the full system in our Lore section.

Where can I read Shadow Slave?

Shadow Slave is available on Webnovel and Amazon Kindle. Please support the author Guiltythree!

What is RIFT?

RIFT is a free companion web app for Shadow Slave readers. It includes a character encyclopedia, AI chapter recaps, lore guides, RPG inventory tracker, world map, and community discussions. It's the #1 tool for Shadow Slave fans — built by a reader who was tired of forgetting who everyone was by chapter 800.

How many chapters does Shadow Slave have?

Shadow Slave has over 2,400 chapters as of 2026 and is still ongoing. RIFT tracks all chapters and provides AI-powered recaps so you never fall behind.
